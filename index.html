About Me


My name is Ishaan, a self taught artist with over 10+ years of experience. My work transforms raw emotions into meaningful visual experiences. Through conceptual art, dark art, and comic art, I explore themes of grief, longing, and spiritual love, while also bringing stories to life through strong and stylized characters.


My work blends symbolism, emotional depth, and visual storytelling. Each artwork is created to help viewers connect with the work on personal, and also on philosophical level. The intention is to encourage reflection and deeper understanding. Fluent in English, Hindi, Urdu, Arabic, and Persian, which allows me to communicate with diverse audiences and share the essence of my work across different cultures.

Art Expertise

  • Dark Art — Emotional depth, grief & melancholy themes
