Perspective

Why lived understanding matters more in art education..??


There are two kinds of qualifications: One is personal qualification, and the other is academic qualification. When it comes to intellectual or philosophical matters, personal qualification holds more importance than academic qualification. Personal qualification is based on experience, knowledge, and records, things that can be verified, and in many cases, it can be more authentic than academic qualifications. This is because it is often seen that even people who have completed a postgraduate degree in Hindi many a times mispronounce basic words, for example, saying “sareer” instead of “shareer.” Even if they don’t have any knowledge, they will still be considered postgraduates because they have the academic qualification, that is, degree.
