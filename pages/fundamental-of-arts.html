Course Overview

Fundamentals of Art — 7 Day Course


In today’s time, learning how to draw is easy. But drawing with accuracy, confidence, and understanding is not. Most artists struggle not because of a lack of talent, but because their fundamentals are weak.


This course is designed to build a strong foundation in art. Over 7 days, you will learn essential skills like line control, shapes and forms, perspective, light and shadow, proportion, and composition.


Instead of guessing or copying, you will learn how to observe, construct, and draw with clarity. These are the core principles that every artist needs, no matter their style.


By the end of the course, you will not only draw better, but also understand how drawing works — giving you the confidence to create your own work.

Because without fundamentals…
even the best ideas fall apart.
