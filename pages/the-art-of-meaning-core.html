The Art of Meaning (Basic to Advance) - A 14-Day Original Art Course






This course is not just about copying better. It's about learning how to study a reference And how to implement it while making original art.


The Art of Meaning (Basic to Advance) is a 14-day Original Art-making course where you won't just copy references. You'll learn to create original art, from absolute basics to an advanced level. Art that expresses feelings, emotions, ideas, creativity, concept & composition, storytelling, and truth.

Over these 14 days, you'll learn the kind of drawing from basic to advanced that defines a real artist, not someone who just makes hand-traced copies. • You want people to remember you by your work
• You don't want to be "just one of them"
• You want a unique identity as an artist
